# Redeemly – Vision, Mission & Values

## Vision
To create a Christ-centered digital community where people pursuing Jesus are supported, encouraged, and discipled through truth, grace, and community.

## Mission
Redeemly exists to provide a safe, moderated, biblically grounded online space for people walking toward freedom and discipleship in Jesus Christ—together, not alone.

## Core Values
- Jesus-centered discipleship
- Truth spoken in love
- Grace without compromise
- Safety, dignity, and respect
- Community over isolation
- Accountability with compassion

## Statement of Faith (Short Form)
Redeemly affirms historic, orthodox Christianity:  
Salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ, the authority of Scripture, repentance, transformation through the Holy Spirit, and life lived in obedience to Christ.
