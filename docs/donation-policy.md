# Donation Policy
**Last Updated:** [Insert Date]

Redeemly is a nonprofit ministry organization based in the State of Tennessee. Donations made through the Redeemly platform support our mission and operations.

---

## 1. Donation Processing
Donations are processed through third-party payment providers. Redeemly does not store or process full payment card information on its own servers.

Processing fees may be deducted by the payment provider unless otherwise stated.

---

## 2. Tax-Exempt Status
Redeemly intends to operate as a nonprofit organization. Donation tax deductibility depends on Redeemly’s tax-exempt status under applicable law and the donor’s individual circumstances.

Donors are responsible for consulting a tax professional regarding deductibility.

---

## 3. Refund Policy
Donations are generally non-refundable. Refund requests may be considered on a case-by-case basis and are not guaranteed.

If a refund is approved, it will be issued using the original payment method when possible.

---

## 4. Use of Funds
Donations are used to support Redeemly’s mission, including platform development, hosting, moderation, content creation, and ministry operations.

Redeemly reserves the right to allocate funds where they are most needed to fulfill its mission.

---
