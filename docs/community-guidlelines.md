# Community Guidelines

## Purpose
Redeemly exists for discipleship, encouragement, prayer, and spiritual growth.

## Encouraged
- Prayer and Scripture
- Honest testimony
- Respectful support
- Accountability

## Not Allowed
- Harassment or slurs
- Sexual content or solicitation
- Pornography
- Threats or violence
- Spam or impersonation

## Sensitive Topics
- Experiences may be shared respectfully
- No medical or therapy claims
- Encourage professional help when needed

## Enforcement
- Reporting system
- Warnings → suspension → ban
- Appeal process available
