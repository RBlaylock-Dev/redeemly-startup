# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Problem
Many people seeking to follow Jesus lack safe, faith-aligned community and discipleship resources online.

## Goals
- Provide community, accountability, and encouragement
- Offer curated Bible studies and resources
- Enable Scripture lookup and saving
- Maintain strong moderation and privacy

## MVP Features
### Authentication & Profiles
- Email/social login
- User profiles with privacy controls
- Optional testimony section

### Community
- Post feed
- Comments
- Reactions (Amen, Prayed, Encouraged)
- Groups (public/private)

### Resources
- Resource library
- Categories & tags
- Bookmarking

### Scripture
- Bible verse lookup
- Save & share verses

### Safety & Moderation
- Reporting system
- Block users
- Admin moderation dashboard

## Non-Goals
- Dating
- Public debates
- Replacement for church leadership or counseling

## Success Metrics
- User activation (profile + group joined)
- Retention
- Safety response time
